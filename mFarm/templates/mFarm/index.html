About MFarm

At Milk Procurement Application, we believe that farmers are the backbone of our society, and we are committed to supporting them in every way possible. Our mission is to remove the exploitation of farmers by middlemen and create a fair and transparent milk procurement system. We have developed a mobile app that connects farmers directly to milk societies and processing companies, ensuring that they receive fair prices for their milk produce.
